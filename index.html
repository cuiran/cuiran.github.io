Welcome!

I am a Ph.D. student at University of Maryland. My advisor is Jeffrey Adams. I am graduating in May 2016.

I study representation theory and Lie groups. Especially the Real-Quaternionic indicator of irreducible self-conjugate representations and how it relates to the Frobenius-Schur indicator. I'm interested in related concepts in p-adic representation theory. I'm also interested in the p-adic Langlands program and the atlas of Lie groups project.

I am also a beginner Data Scientist. I'm interested in machine learning, artificial intelligence, and the application of representation theory in probability and statistics.
