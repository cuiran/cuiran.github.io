Welcome to my webpage! I am a math PhD student at University of Maryland, my advisor is Jeffrey Adams.

Research Interest

I'm interested in representation theory, specifically the Real-Quaternionic indicator and the Frobenius-Schur of representations of real Lie groups, as well as related concepts in p-adic Lie groups. I'm also interested in the p-adic Langlands program.
